Sally's Banana Bread French Toast

Sally brought this recipe into the family when she was first invited to the sunday brunch. In true Watson style, Wilbur gave his girlfriend a beautiful green sundress, to show her off in while she met the family. Everyone loved her almost instantly, but what won her over with Ma and the Aunts was this delicious french toast. All of the kids loved it, and she made sure to cut it into small pieces for the lil'uns. She's a Watson family prize, that's for sure.

Ingreedients

  • 3 Tablespoons Sweetened Condensed Milk
  • 1 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract
  • 2 Tablespoons Butter
  • 1 Loaf Banana Bread
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon confectioners sugar for dusting

Directions

  1. In a shallow bowl, whisk together the eggs, sweetened condensed milk and vanilla with a fork. Set aside.
  2. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Slice banana bread into 4 thick slices. Dip each slice into the egg mixture, then place in the hot pan. Cook on each side until golden brown. Dust with confectioners' sugar just before serving, if desired. Pour syrup on it for the liluns'
